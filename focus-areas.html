Focus Areas

Where ASC is especially interested in contributing

The categories below describe the kinds of challenges and collaborations that align especially well with ASC’s mission and technical orientation.

Preparedness & Resilience

ASC is interested in tools, frameworks, and collaborative efforts that help communities and organizations understand capability gaps, assess readiness, and strengthen resilience in the face of disruption.

Cybersecurity & Risk Awareness

We are interested in educational tools, simulations, and decision-support approaches that help improve awareness of cyber risk and strengthen the capacity of organizations and communities to respond effectively.

Applied AI for Public Benefit

Artificial intelligence can be useful in support of analysis, planning, learning, and mission-driven problem solving. ASC is interested in responsible uses of AI that are practical, explainable, and aligned with human needs rather than novelty alone.

Collaborative Solution Design

Some of the most promising work happens when technical expertise is paired with deep domain understanding and lived experience. ASC is interested in projects where meaningful collaboration can produce tools or approaches that are genuinely useful and sustainable.

Future Areas of Exploration

As ASC grows, additional areas of mission-aligned exploration may emerge. The organization intends to remain open to new opportunities where advanced technology can be applied responsibly in support of humanitarian and public-interest needs.

Mission Fit Matters

Not every technical problem is an ASC problem. We are especially interested in projects where mission alignment, practical value, and responsible use are all clearly present.
