Humanitarian Technology

Advanced technology in service of public benefit.

Adaptive Solutions Collaborative, Inc. is a nonprofit organization that works with mission-aligned collaborators to design, develop, and deploy practical technology solutions for humanitarian and public-interest challenges.

Explore Collaboration Learn More

What We Do

ASC exists to help bridge the gap between meaningful mission needs and the thoughtful use of advanced technologies. We collaborate with organizations that understand the problem space and seek practical, responsible, and effective solutions that serve people and communities.

Focus Areas

Preparedness & Resilience

Tools and approaches that help communities and organizations better understand capability gaps and strengthen readiness.

Cybersecurity & Risk Awareness

Educational and simulation-based resources that improve risk awareness, preparedness, and decision support.

Applied AI for Public Benefit

Responsible uses of artificial intelligence to support learning, planning, analysis, and mission-focused problem solving.

Collaborative Solution Design

Partnerships that combine domain expertise, lived experience, and technical capability to produce useful and sustainable outcomes.

Why ASC

Adaptive Solutions Collaborative is designed to work at the intersection of technical expertise and humanitarian purpose. We aim to help organizations explore practical solutions that might otherwise remain out of reach due to limited technical capacity, limited time, or insufficient resources.

Ways to Engage

Philanthropic Institutions

Foundations and grantmakers interested in practical, technology-enabled approaches to humanitarian challenges.

Nonprofits & Community Organizations

Organizations with deep understanding of mission needs and real-world challenges that may benefit from technical collaboration.

Researchers & Technologists

People interested in applying advanced technical skills in support of mission-driven, public-interest work.

Donors & Supporters

Individuals and institutions whose support can help bring practical technical capabilities to important mission areas.
