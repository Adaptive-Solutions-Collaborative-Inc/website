Adaptive Solutions Collaborative, Inc. is a nonprofit organization that applies advanced and disruptive technologies in support of humanitarian, resilience, preparedness, and public-interest work.

Explore Collaboration Learn More

Mission

Our mission is to use advanced and disruptive technologies for humanitarian purposes by collaborating with organizations that bring meaningful challenges, domain knowledge, and mission alignment.

Focus Areas

Preparedness & Resilience

Tools and approaches that help communities and organizations better understand capability gaps and strengthen readiness.

Cybersecurity & Awareness

Educational and simulation-based resources that improve risk awareness, preparedness, and decision support.

Applied AI for Public Benefit

Responsible use of AI to support analysis, learning, planning, and mission-focused problem solving.

Collaborative Solution Design

Partnerships that combine technical expertise with real-world needs to create practical, sustainable solutions.

Ways to Engage

We welcome conversations with philanthropic institutions, nonprofit organizations, researchers, technologists, and donors whose goals align with our mission.
