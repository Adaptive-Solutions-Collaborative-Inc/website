Transparency

Governance, responsibility, and trust

ASC seeks to build credibility through mission alignment, practical work, thoughtful governance, and transparent communication.

Independence and Governance

Adaptive Solutions Collaborative operates as an independent nonprofit organization. Project selection, mission direction, and organizational priorities are intended to be governed in service of the nonprofit mission and public benefit.

Responsible Technology

ASC is committed to the ethical and responsible application of advanced technology in humanitarian and public-interest contexts. We believe that technical capability alone is not sufficient; solutions should also be practical, proportionate, understandable, and aligned with human needs.

Donated Tools and Contributed Artifacts

ASC may receive donated tools, research artifacts, or technical contributions from aligned collaborators. Such contributions are intended to support ASC’s nonprofit mission and are expected to be evaluated and used in ways consistent with that mission and with appropriate governance.

Aligned Collaborators

ASC may collaborate with mission-aligned organizations, researchers, technical contributors, and supporters. Collaboration does not, by itself, imply organizational control, exclusive relationship, or deviation from ASC’s independent nonprofit purpose.

Institutional Trust

We recognize that potential collaborators, donors, and grant reviewers need confidence that ASC is serious, thoughtful, and trustworthy. This site is one part of that effort. As ASC grows, this section may expand to include additional governance, reporting, or policy materials.

Questions or Institutional Inquiries

If you would like to discuss governance, collaboration, or mission fit, please reach out directly.
